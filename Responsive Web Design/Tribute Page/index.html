This is a recopilation of some information about his life. To see more information, you can go to the Wikipedia article about his life.

1856

Nikola Tesla born in the Austrian Empire (actually Croatia) on 10 July.

1859

At the age of 3, he was caressing the back of his cat while he felt sparks on his hand. He wanted to know what was that strange phenomenon, when he asked his father and him let Nikola Tesla know the feeling he was experiencing was called electricity. Thanks to this event, he interested in the electricity.

1873

At the age of 17, he contracted cholera, and was very close to the death. His father promised him he will send Nikola Tesla to the best engineering school when he recovered from the illness. This made Nikola extremely happy, as he was interested in this topic while he was a kid and a teen.

1875-1880

In 1875, he enrolled on a military scolarship, where he studied a lot about electricity, read a lot of books and continued learning. As he said, he studied for 20 hours a day, sleeping only 4 hours.
Nikola Tesla was suffering from nervous breakdown. He left school and became gambling. He lost and gained money, but it wasn't worth.
In 1879, his father died.
In 1880, two of Tesla's uncles put together some money to help him enroll a university again and start studying. He entered in the Charles-Ferdinand University. He attended engineering classes but also philosphy classes.

1882

Nikola Tesla got a job in the Continental Edison Company, in Paris. He gained a lot of experience in electrical engineering.

1884

In this year, Nikola Tesla moved to United States to manage the Edison Machine Works. Six months later, he left the company.
The next year, Nikola Tesla kept working on electric engineering, this time working on the Tesla Electric Light & Manufacturing.

1889

This year is what started the real career of Nikola Tesla.
He worked on several projects, including Tesla Coil, Wireless Lighting, Polyphase System, Steam-powered Oscilating Generator, The Nikola Tesla Company, X-ray Experimentation and Radio Remote Control. He met a lot of people who helped him with these projects.

1906

In this time Nikola Tesla worked with several companies who needed his help. He did made the Bladeless Turbine and the Wireless Lawsuits.

1931

A science-fiction writer organized a celebration for the 75 birthday of Nikola Tesla. He received a lot of letters for known scientists. Albert Einstein also recognized his work, and he appeared in the cover of Time Magazine.
Later, he worked with X-ray again and claimed he discovered cosmic rays.

1943

Died in New York, United States, on 7 January.

Nikola Tesla is an incredible person. He is known for his works in the electric and electromagnetism field.
